Limoncello

Opening a bottle of limoncello has a transporting effect. Like stepping into a sun-drenched citrus grove, the clouds part and the golden liquid is a temporary reminder that sunshine can indeed survive indoors.

Ingredients:

  • 10 Organic Lemons
  • 750ml Vodka
  • 1 to 4 Cups Sugar

How To Make:

  1. Peel the lemons and cover the peels with vodka
  2. Set the vodka and lemon peels infuse somewhere out of the way and out of direct sunlight for at least 4 days or as long as a month
  3. Strain the infused vodka through the filter
  4. Prepare a sugar syrup of at least 1 cup of water and 1 cup of sugar â€” bring the water to a simmer and stir in the sugar to dissolve; allow to cool
  5. Mix the sugar syrup with the infused vodka
  6. Insert the funnel in the neck of one of the bottles and fill with limoncello. Repeat with remaining bottles
  7. Chill the limoncello in the fridge or freezer for at least 4 hours before drinking
