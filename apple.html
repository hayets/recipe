Apple Spice Smoothie

A creamy Apple Spice Smoothie is just what the doctor is ordering this fall season. A creamy apple smoothie that is rich, creamy, and downright a sweet treat. You can also use soy milk for a dairy-free smoothie recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Apple
  • Banana
  • Milk or Soy Milk
  • Cinnamon Powder
  • Oats
  • Protein Powder (opeitonal)

How To Make:

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth
  2. Sprinkle extra cinnamon on top before serving, if desired
