Homemade Hot Sauce

Hot sauce has been shown to prevent obesity and help people to maintain healthy weights. It reduces levels of ghrelin, a hormone that triggers hunger, and it raises levels of an appetite-suppressing hormone called GLP-1, which will help prevent you from overeating.

Ingredients:

  • Hot Peppers
  • Aromatic Veggies (like garlic or onion)

How To Make:

  1. Combine salt with warm, filtered water to create a brine
  2. Fill a jar with peppers and garlic
  3. Cover with brine
  4. Cover the jar with a fermentation lid or cheesecloth, and let ferment for 5-7 days (or until the brine looks cloudy)
